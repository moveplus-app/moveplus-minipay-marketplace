Move+ Marketplace Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

Scope

This policy describes how Move+ / Amaya handles information collected through the Move+ Web Marketplace checkout and order flow. It supplements the main Move+ app privacy practices and focuses on Real Items orders and cUSD MiniPay payments on this site.

Information we collect

For Marketplace checkout and fulfillment, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Delivery address and delivery comments
  • Selected items and order details
  • Payment transaction hash and related payment verification data

How we use this information

  • Order processing and delivery coordination
  • Customer support and order status communication
  • Fraud prevention and abuse detection
  • On-chain payment verification for cUSD MiniPay checkout

Blockchain & MiniPay payments

cUSD transactions on Celo are public on the blockchain. Move+ does not put your name, phone, email, or delivery address on-chain.

We may process your MiniPay/Celo wallet address and payment transaction hash to verify that payment was received for your order. This verification is separate from Energy balance or in-app Energy redemption.

Storage & security

Delivery and order details are stored off-chain in Move+ backend systems (Supabase). Service role keys and other privileged credentials are never exposed to the Marketplace frontend. Only public configuration (such as the Supabase anon key) is used in the browser for catalog access.

Your choices

You can contact Move+ Support to request help with privacy questions, order data corrections, or deletion requests where applicable and permitted by law.
