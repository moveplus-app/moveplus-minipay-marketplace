Move+ Marketplace Refund Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

cUSD / MiniPay payments (Real Items)

  • If you paid with cUSD through MiniPay and Move+ cannot fulfill your order (for example due to stock or supplier availability), you may request a support review.
  • Refunds are processed manually in V1. Please allow time for review and processing.
  • Approved refunds are sent back to the original paying wallet where technically and operationally possible.

Delivery issues

Claims for damaged, incorrect, or missing items must be reported within 48 hours after you receive the delivery. Include your order details and photos where applicable when contacting support.

Energy redemption (Move+ app)

Energy redemption for marketplace items inside the Move+ mobile app follows the main Move+ app marketplace and Energy policies. MiniPay cUSD payment on this web Marketplace and in-app Energy redemption are separate paths — paying with cUSD here does not affect your Energy balance, and redeeming with Energy in the app does not replace a cUSD checkout on this site.

How to request help

Contact Move+ Support with your order reference, payment transaction hash (for cUSD orders), and delivery details. Our team will review eligible refund or replacement requests.
